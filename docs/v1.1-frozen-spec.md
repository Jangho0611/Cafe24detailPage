# Cafe24 Detail Generator v1.1 Frozen Spec

## Project Philosophy

Cafe24 Detail Generator v1.1 is not a project for making SEO pages.

The goal is to create construction-material content that buyers can understand easily. As a result, the shopping mall detail page and blog should become reliable source material for Google AI Overview, Naver AI, ChatGPT Search, and Perplexity.

If the content feels like it was written by AI, it fails.

Core principles:
- Put the buyer before the search engine.
- Explanation comes before advertising.
- A page that feels AI-written is not acceptable.
- Aim for the tone of a construction-material consultant with 35 years of field experience.
- Content that is easy for people to read is also easier for AI systems to understand.
- SEO is an outcome. The purpose is trustworthy construction-material content.
- New features are added to v1.1 only when they improve both human understanding and AI understanding.

## Content Quality Rule

Content Quality Score is not only a post-generation QA metric. It is also the target quality standard before generation.

Generation logic must be designed to satisfy the Content Quality Rule from the start:
- Generate from verified entity data.
- Write for search intent, not keyword stuffing.
- Use human consulting language.
- Avoid unsupported claims.
- Keep body content and schema consistent.
- Prevent duplicated meaning across sections.

## Content Quality Score

Total: 100 points.

| Area | Points | Evaluation |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Human Writing | 15 | Natural consulting tone, no AI-like phrasing, no translationese |
| Search Intent | 10 | Answers real buyer/search questions naturally |
| AI Summary | 10 | Summarizes product, use, and pre-order checks in 3 sentences or fewer |
| Product Definition | 10 | Defines the product clearly without exaggeration |
| FAQ | 10 | Uses product-group search intent and direct answers |
| Schema | 10 | Product, FAQPage, Breadcrumb, and properties match visible content |
| Entity Consistency | 10 | Product, material, size, use, installation, comparison, and cautions do not conflict |
| Trustworthiness | 10 | No unsupported facts, no exaggeration, no entity/schema mismatch |
| Readability | 5 | Easy to read aloud and understand |
| Duplication | 10 | AI Summary, Define, ds-reason, FAQ, and Schema do not repeat the same sentence or meaning |

Operation criteria:
- 85 or higher: ready for operation.
- 75 to 84: review recommended.
- 74 or lower: regenerate or revise.

Mandatory failure conditions:
- Unsupported performance, certification, price, inventory, rating, or review claims.
- Exaggerated or promotional wording.
- Strong AI-like phrasing.
- Duplicate FAQ and ds-reason sentences.
- Body and Schema mismatch.
- Product-group misclassification.
- Incorrect comparison-product information.
- Forbidden expressions such as "선택됩니다", "활용됩니다", "최적", "우수한", or "프리미엄".

## One Source of Truth

v1.1 follows this rule:

One Fact -> Many Expressions.

All content must be derived from one shared Entity Data source. New facts must not be invented in individual sections.

```text
Entity Data
  - Product
  - Product group
  - Material
  - Structure
  - Size
  - Thickness
  - Maker
  - Certification/performance status
  - Use
  - Installation/cutting cautions
  - Comparison product
  - Pre-order checks
  - Source or evidence status

        ↓

AI Summary
Product Definition
ds-reason
FAQ
Schema
Blog
```

Rules:
- AI Summary, Define, ds-reason, FAQ, Schema, and Blog all come from the same Entity Data.
- The same fact may be expressed differently by section, but the fact itself must not change.
- New facts must not be created inside prompts, post-processing, Schema, or blog links.
- Body content and Schema must not disagree.
- Schema must not include claims that are not visible or supported in the body/entity data.
- Blog content may expand the explanation, but must still point back to the same entity facts.

## Section Roles

### AI Summary
- Role: top-level buyer summary.
- 3 sentences or fewer.
- Covers product identity, main use, and pre-order checks.
- Must be easy to quote and easy to read.

### Product Definition
- Role: define what the product is.
- Focus on material, structure, and composition.
- Do not repeat the AI Summary sentence.

### ds-reason
- Role: purchase-context information.
- Covers structure, usage reason, installation notes, and pre-order checks.
- Must not reuse FAQ sentences.

### FAQ
- Role: answer search-intent questions directly.
- Questions are search-like.
- Answers are consulting-like.
- Product-group FAQ takes priority over generic FAQ.

### Schema
- Role: machine-readable reflection of visible content.
- Product, FAQPage, Breadcrumb, and additionalProperty must match the body.
- Price, inventory, rating, review, and unsupported offer data are excluded.

### Blog
- Role: explain comparison, guide, installation, and problem-solving topics.
- Blog links support the detail page, not keyword stuffing.
- Blog and detail page should share entity facts.

## Human Writing Rule

Common rules:
- Do not repeat the same sentence ending more than twice in a row.
- Use "및" no more than once per sentence.
- Avoid translationese and stiff passive phrasing.
- One sentence should contain one main piece of information.
- Vary sentence length.
- Use a tone that a construction-material consultant could say to a customer.

Forbidden expressions:
- 선택됩니다
- 활용됩니다
- 활용할 수 있습니다
- 사용 작업
- 활용 작업
- 최적
- 우수한
- 뛰어난
- 효율적
- 프리미엄
- 고급
- 최고급
- 다양한 용도
- 적합합니다
- 제공합니다
- 확인할 수 있습니다
- 일반적으로

Preferred expressions:
- 많이 사용합니다.
- 찾는 경우가 많습니다.
- 먼저 확인하는 것이 좋습니다.
- 현장 조건에 맞춰 확인합니다.
- 실내 마감 바탕재로 많이 사용합니다.
- 재단 전 치수를 다시 보는 편이 좋습니다.
- 표면 상태를 보고 결정하는 것이 좋습니다.

## Search Intent Rule

v1.1 does not generate around SEO keywords. It generates around search intent.

Keywords should appear naturally while answering the buyer's question.

Examples:
- 합판: where it is used, MDF difference, indoor/outdoor use, cutting cautions.
- MDF: what it is, PB difference, painting and wrapping checks.
- 석고보드: general vs waterproof board, where it is used, joint treatment.
- PF보드: insulation use, cutting cautions, joint and finish checks.
- 각재: framing use, cutting length, exposed-face checks.

## QA Checklist

Generated content must be checked with these questions:
- Is it natural for a person to read?
- Would a construction-material consultant with 35 years of experience say this to a customer?
- Does it feel AI-written?
- Is there translationese?
- Does explanation come before advertising?
- Does it satisfy the search intent?
- Are sentence endings repeated?
- Is "및" overused?
- Do AI Summary, Define, ds-reason, FAQ, and Schema avoid duplicated meaning?
- Do Product, FAQPage, Breadcrumb, and properties match the visible body?
- Are product, material, size, use, installation, comparison product, and cautions consistent?
- Are unsupported facts excluded?
